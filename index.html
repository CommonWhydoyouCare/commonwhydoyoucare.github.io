CSP World Congress 2023

Where?

Presentations, accommodation, and meals will take place at the Hotel Regitnig next to the Weissensee, Austria. Information on how to arrive there will be given here.

When?

The congress will take place from Monday 18 September to Friday 22 September. Arrival is on Sunday, departure on Saturday morning.

How to attend?

As every year, participation is by invitation only. However if you did not receive an invitation by email and wish to participate, you can email the organizers.
